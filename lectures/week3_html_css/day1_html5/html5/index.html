Block elements

Nav

Defines a block containing site or page navigation. <nav>

Header

The heading of the block that it is contained within. I.e. within an article tag it is the heading of the article. <header>

Hgroup

A group of heading text. Often a main title and a slogen.

<hgroup>
  <h1>Hallstahammars kommun</h1>
  <h2>Almost as good as Västerås</h2>
</hgroup>

Article

An article for example in a paper or blog. The outermost tag containing all the information about a specific article i.e. header, p, img, figures, footer etc. <article>

Footer

A footer appears often at the bottom of a page or article. They are often used for meta data. i.e. author, contact information, copyright.
